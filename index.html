ABOUT

Amelia Zhang is a Chinese student studying at Otago Girls’ High School. Her passion for the arts of fashion and design began at the age of 11 and her interest in illustration started ever since she could remember. Entranced by the idea of turning abstract sketches into real life creations, her ambition and enthusiasm has only grown since her first encounter with a sewing machine. In July of 2022, Zhang’s entry for the Hokonui Fashion Design Awards was featured on the Hokonui Fashion Design Awards runway show. Receiving a Merit award, this placed her garment in the top five of her section; Glamour (School). Zhang’s intertwining of Chinese and Western culture remains frequent in her work and she is at her early stages of discovering her sense of style in fashion design.
